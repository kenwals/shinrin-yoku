So what is Shinrin Yoku?

It is the Japanese for Forest Bathing, Forest Bathing is an accepted part of Japanese preventative health care since the 1980s.

Since then it has been practiced worldwide and researchers continue to investigate its outcomes. It is widely considered a powerful antidote to the pressures of the modern world, proven to deliver lasting benefits to your physical and mental well-being, including: improved sleep, reduced stress, increased immunity, lower blood pressure and accelerated recovery from illness or trauma.

If you've ever moved slowly through a forest, listened to the wind in the trees or noticed the sunshine filtering through the leaves, then you've already practised it in your own way!

If you are looking to deepen this experience and explore your connection to nature, this is where our fully qualified Forest Bathing Guide, Sophia , comes in. She will guide you on a gentle walk using a series of invitations that will awaken your senses to focus on the natural environment around you. We aim to help you develop an enhanced sense of appreciation for nature and to re-balance your body and mind.
